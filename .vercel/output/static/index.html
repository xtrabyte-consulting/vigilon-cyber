Our Core Services

End-to-end cybersecurity compliance solutions for connected products entering the European market.

RED Cyber CE Mark Testing

Full compliance testing against EN 18031 for conformity to RED DA Article 3.3 d/e/f now required for CE marking.

Cyber Resilience Act

Prepare your products for the EU Cyber Resilience Act requirements coming in 2027, including vulnerability handling, SBOM management and ongoing obligations.

ETSI IoT Security

Assessment against ETSI EN 303 645 — the baseline cybersecurity standard for consumer IoT devices entering the European market.

Penetration Testing

Regular penetration tests across web applications, APIs, mobile apps, IoT devices and cloud infrastructure to uncover exploitable vulnerabilities.

Risk Assessment & Threat Modelling

Structured risk assessments and threat modelling to identify, prioritise and mitigate security risks before they become compliance blockers.

Vulnerability Scanning

Automated and manual vulnerability scanning services to continuously monitor your attack surface and maintain your security posture.

Why Choose Vigilon Cyber?

We specialise exclusively in cybersecurity compliance for products entering new markets — it's all we do. Our clients stick with Vigilon because if your product doesn't pass the first time, we will continue to provide expert guidance, remediation support and re-tests until the your product is market ready.

Regulatory Expertise

Deep knowledge of the Radio Equipment Directive, EN 18031 series, NIST Cybersecurity and Cryptography Publications, and ETSI EN 303 645. We stay ahead of evolving requirements so you don't have to.

Dual-Path Support

Whether you're pursuing self-declaration or preparing for a Notified Body assessment, we tailor our approach to your compliance pathway.

Actionable Reporting

Our reports don't just list findings — they provide clear remediation guidance prioritised by risk, so your engineering team can act fast.
